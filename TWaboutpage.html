In 1430, Zheng He (or Cheng Ho) landed on the island and obtained herbal medicines that were stated by the natives to have "miracle power."

Despite this, the Portuguese did not claimed Taiwan and the Dutch would established theirs later on. Under the Empire of the Rising Sun, Taiwan saw some advancement in its economy and science. Taiwan would particpart in World War 2 that would see the deaths of 30,000 soldiers in combat roles. It was left alone by the Allies though U.S. air raids did damage the oil-storage depots. After the end of the war, Taiwan was handed back to the Kuomintang government under Chiang Kai-shek. However after the Nationaltist defeat from the Communist from the Chinese Civil War, the remaining ones loyal to the Republic of China moved to Taiwan.
